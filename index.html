Here are some of my recent projects...

CDSS Data & Tools

The Colorado Decision Support System (CDSS) is a collection of datasets & tools provided by the Colorado Division of Water Resources (DWR) & Colorado Water Convservation Board (CWCB).


HBDMC

The Hydrobase Data Management Console (HBDMC) is a Windows Presentation Foundation (WPF) application used to perform internal business functions, including permitting, structure analysis, meter readings, & dam safety for the Colorado Division of Water Resources (DWR) & Colorado Water Convservation Board (CWCB).


DNR Portal

The Department of Natural Resources (DNR) Portal is a single sign-on (SSO) application to handle authentication for the Colorado Division of Water Resources (DWR), Colorado Water Convservation Board (CWCB), & Oil & Gas Conservation Commission (OGCC).


SDPC Permitting & Licensing System

The South Dakota Plumbing Commission (SDPC) Permitting & Licensing System allows users to request & pay for permits, licenses, & exam registrations.


SD UI ICON

The South Dakota Unemployment Insurance (UI) Interstate Connection Network (ICON) is a Simple Object Access Protocol (SOAP) service for tracking interstate benefits, claims, & wages.


SDEC

The South Dakota Electrical Commission (SDEC) Console is a Windows Forms application used to manage licenses, permits, & inspections for the SDEC.
