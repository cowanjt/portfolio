Here are some of my recent projects...

Colorado Decision Support System

The Colorado Decision Support System (CDSS) is a collection of datasets and tools provided by the Colorado Division of Water Resources (DWR) and Colorado Water Convservation Board (CWCB).


Hydrobase Data Management Console

The Hydrobase Data Management Console (HBDMC) is a Windows Presentation Foundation (WPF) application that handles internal business functions, including permitting, structure analysis, meter readings, and dam safety for the Colorado Division of Water Resources (DWR) and Colorado Water Convservation Board (CWCB).


Department of Natural Resources Portal

The Department of Natural Resources (DNR) Portal is a single sign-on (SSO) application handling authentication for the Colorado Division of Water Resources (DWR), Colorado Water Convservation Board (CWCB), and Oil and Gas Conservation Commission (OGCC).


Colorado Decision Support System REST Service

The Colorado Decision Support System (CDSS) Representational State Transfer (REST) service provides a programmatic way to access HydroBase data.
